Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What services do you offer for Thanksgiving?
We offer Thanksgiving dinner cleanup, pre-event cleaning, post-event cleanup, and house preparation services.

How much does Thanksgiving dinner cleanup cost?
Thanksgiving dinner cleanup starts at $100, but pricing may vary based on the size of the event and specific requirements.

Do you provide cleaning services before and after the Thanksgiving event?
Yes, we offer pre-event cleaning to prepare your home for guests and post-event cleanup to ensure everything is spotless afterward.

How can I schedule your Thanksgiving services?
You can schedule our Thanksgiving services by contacting us through our website or giving us a call.

Are your Thanksgiving services available on holidays?
Yes, we understand the importance of Thanksgiving, so our services are available during the holiday season.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies for your services?
No, we bring our own cleaning supplies and equipment to ensure thorough and professional service.
